About FundedIn

Empowering traders to reach their full potential with capital and cutting-edge technology

Our Values

Community First

FundedIn is built around traders. Decisions are driven by long-term community health over short-term revenue. FundedIn evolve from real trader behavior, feedback, and performance data. If a choice benefits growth but harms trader trust or learning, it is rejected.

Transparency & Trust

Open communication, clear expectations, honest progress updates. Nothing is hidden. No ambiguous value promises, and overhyped claims.

Accountability

FundedIn takes responsibility for outcomes it controls: clear rules, enforced risk limits, consistent evaluations, and no discretionary manipulation. If a trader passes or fails, the reason is explicit, measurable, and reproducible.

Our Vision

FundedIn aims to build India’s most credible trader ecosystem. A community of high performing retail traders. And help them scale into professional market participants without predatory structures or opaque incentives.

We envision a future where talented traders are not limited by capital constraints but are empowered to showcase their skills and earn what they truly deserve. By bridging the gap between skill and capital, we're creating a new paradigm in the Indian trading ecosystem.
