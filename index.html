Frequently Asked Questions

FundedIn is a premium trading infrastructure that evaluates traders. Successful traders get access to real capital to trade the markets using our institutional-grade platform.

Simply select an evaluation account size that suits you, pass the trading objectives (profit target and loss limits), and you will be eligible for a funded account with real capital.

Our rules are simple and designed to protect capital. The main rules are the Maximum Loss limit (10%) and Daily Loss limit (5%). We also require responsible risk management practices.

Payouts are processed on demand or bi-weekly depending on your account stage. You keep up to 80% of the profits you generate.
