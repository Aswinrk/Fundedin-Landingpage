Risk Disclosure

Trading equities, derivatives, and related instruments involves substantial risk. Review this disclosure before participating in any evaluation or funded program.

Effective date: February 20, 2026

1. Market Risk

Financial markets can be volatile and move rapidly. Prices may gap or move against your position without warning, leading to significant losses.

2. Leverage and Derivatives Risk

Leverage can magnify gains and losses. Derivative products such as options and futures may carry complex risk characteristics that can result in losses greater than expected.

3. Execution and Technology Risk

System outages, connectivity interruptions, exchange issues, delayed data, or order execution delays may affect trading outcomes and risk controls.

4. Liquidity Risk

Some instruments may have limited liquidity, resulting in slippage or inability to enter or exit positions at desired prices.

5. Strategy and Behavioral Risk

Even proven strategies can fail under changing market conditions. Emotional decision-making, overtrading, and poor discipline can increase losses.

6. No Guaranteed Returns

Past performance is not indicative of future results. Participation in evaluation or funded programs does not guarantee payouts or profitability.

7. Regulatory and Tax Considerations

You are responsible for understanding and complying with laws, regulations, and tax obligations applicable in your jurisdiction.

8. Acknowledgment

By using FundedIn services, you acknowledge that you understand these risks and accept full responsibility for your trading decisions.
