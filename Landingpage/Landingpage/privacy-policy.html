Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FundedIn collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website and services.

Effective date: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact details such as name, email, and phone number.
  • Account and verification details required for compliance and payouts.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device identifiers.
  • Usage and trading performance data related to platform services.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and secure the platform.
  • To process onboarding, evaluations, and payouts.
  • To communicate updates, service notices, and support responses.
  • To meet legal, compliance, and fraud prevention obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with trusted service providers and regulatory authorities where required by law. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, satisfy legal and compliance requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No internet-based system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information by contacting us.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience, understand platform performance, and detect misuse.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related requests, email support@fundedin.com.
